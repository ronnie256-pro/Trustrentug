THE PROTOCOL STRUCTURE

The Meaning of T.R.U.S.T.

The five core pillars that define the TRUST Rental Verification Protocol.

T

Title Verification

We verify land titles or ownership documents to ensure the person listing the property is the legitimate owner.

R

Registered Landlord

Every landlord is verified using their National ID (NIN), eliminating anonymous fraud.

U

Urban Inspection

A TRUST Agent physically visits, photographs, and geotags the property to confirm its existence and condition.

S

Secure Escrow

All payments — rent, deposits, and security deposits — are held safely by TRUST until the tenant confirms successful move‑in.

T

Transparent Contract

A legally binding digital tenancy agreement is issued instantly, protecting both parties.

This is how we eliminate fraud from the rental market.
